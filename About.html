Hello, I'm Maria.

a UX Designer from Barcelona.

"Never fall in love with an idea. They’re whores.If the one you’re with isn’t doing the job, there’s always, always, always another" -Chip Kidd

Curious about what makes people tick, I started my journey in the marketing industry because of my interest in understanding buying patterns and attitudes as well as the psychology that encompasses particular marketing techniques that can influence a business.

When I discovered the world of UX, I became fascinated about it (the blend of analysis and design was somehow what I missed, maybe because I’m a Gemini) so I enrolled into a UX design course at Ironhack.

I'm a fast learner, very motivated and I intend to apply user-centered design techniques to deliver long-lasting user experiences.

Other than that, I love traveling, learning from cultures and all sort of music. My motto is adventure yourself into the unknown.

Thanks for the visit.
